About Us

Silver Bells Public School, established in 1999, is a CBSE-affiliated English medium school offering education from Nursery to Class 8. We are dedicated to holistic learning, values, and academic excellence.

Admissions

Who can apply?

  • Students from Nursery to Class 8
  • CBSE curriculum
  • English Medium instruction

How to apply?

  • Visit the school during working hours
  • Bring birth certificate, academic records, photos
